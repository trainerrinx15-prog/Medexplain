How It Works

Three simple steps — from upload to understanding your health data in under a minute.

1
Upload Your Medical Report
Select any medical document from your phone — lab results, X-ray reports, prescriptions, or blood work. Supports PDF and all common image formats (JPG, PNG). Your file is securely transmitted over encrypted connection.
2
AI Reads & Analyzes
Our AI engine, trained on millions of medical documents, reads through the technical terminology and identifies key findings, abnormal values, and important observations in your report — all in under 5 seconds.
3
Get a Clear Summary
Receive a plain-language explanation of what your report means — no jargon, no confusion. Your report and summary are stored securely in your personal account so you can access them anytime.
